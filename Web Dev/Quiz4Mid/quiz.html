About Jollibee

Jollibee, founded in 1978 by Tony Tan Caktiong, is a popular fast-food chain from the Philippines. Known for its popular Filipino-inspired menu featuring fried chicken, spaghetti, and burgers, it has over 1,500 locations worldwide. Jollibee is now part of the Filipino culture and is a staple in every household on any given occassion.
